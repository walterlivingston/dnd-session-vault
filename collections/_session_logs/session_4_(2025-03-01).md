	- [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler.md) transforms into gnome guard
	- [Shirley](/_people/shirley/) is in line to be executed, very short in stature, walking on all fours with fur, but wearing elegant regalia (reinforces that saving her would make us a lot of money)

	- [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler.md) and [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe.md) end up saving [Shirley](/_people/shirley/) and leaving towards [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha.md)'s apartment.

	- Finally, [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) takes the head of the guard and throws it at the feet of the executioner, causing him to flee.
	- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) and [Merla](/_party/merla/) finish fighting
		- [Merla](/_party/merla/) takes [Chua](/_people/chua/)'s parents back to him at the [Yawning Portal](/collections/_locations/Yawning Portal.md)
		- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) goes to [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha.md)'s apartment

	- [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler.md) and [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe.md) have been gifted 100 gold and a baby corgi, who we name [Tilly](/_people/tilly/), one of [Shirley](/_people/shirley/)'s children.

	- [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe.md) asks about what happened to [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) who denies seeing anything, but [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler.md) knows he had a vision.

	- [Erron](/_people/erron/) grabs [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) and as [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) fails to escape his grasp, he reveals hidden secrets and intentions about each of the party members, angering [Merla](/_party/merla/) and [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/).
	
- Party returns to [Yawning Portal](/collections/_locations/Yawning Portal.md)
	- [Erron](/_people/erron/) is visibly upset about the loss of [Kath](/_people/kath/) and is very intoxicated.
	- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) attempts to sedate him with special tea leaves, but is caught and a brawl ensues.

	- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) hits [Erron](/_people/erron/) and watches as orcs join the fight, critically wounding both [Merla](/_party/merla/) and [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe.md).

	- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) then makes an appeal to [Erron](/_people/erron/) who orders the orcs to stand down.
	- In an attempt to make peace, [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) gives [Kath](/_people/kath/)'s amulet to [Erron](/_people/erron/) and asks that the violence stop.
	- [Erron](/_people/erron/) accepts and the party finishes their night by going to sleep.
