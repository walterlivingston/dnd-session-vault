# Violence in Waterdeep

## Timeline
- Awake at the [Yawning Portal](/collections/_locations/Yawning Portal/)
	- Opened gifts found under fir tree
	- [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler/) is ill and [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) slips his gift into his room
	- [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) gets newspaper from [Dilon](/_people/dilon/), reads that many working to lower class families have been killed
- Party goes to the home of [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha/)
	- young child informs party [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha/) has gone to the market
- Travels to Trade Ward
	- busy street, party meets [Greg](/_people/greg/) the half-orc at [Greg's Grogs](/collections/_locations/Greg's Grogs/)
		- [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) says we are servants of a paladin and asks for information about the massacre
		- informed that it was not done with magic and [Greg](/_people/greg/) believes it is the shadow thieves
		- learn that local watch has been pulled from the Trade Ward
- Go to location of massacre
	- nobody around, very bloody, lots of damage, explosive remnants
	- weapons, merch, and bodies, no valuable loot
	- Party is ambushed
		- after defeating them, party learns they were shadow thieves
			- take their robes/patches for possible use later
	- Party continues to investigate
		- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) finds sign with a symbol he recognizes on it
			- [Merla](/_party/merla/) recognizes it as holy and gets similar feeling that she did when meeting [Erron](/_people/erron/)
		- [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) sees symbol on wall that he does not recognize and neither does [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/)
	- Party sees manhole cover and chooses to go down
		- after running into a large monster strangely resembling the hungry hungry caterpillar, quickly go back up the ladder back into the square
		- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) watches as [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) and [Merla](/_party/merla/) feed the monster a poisoned corpse, does not seem to affect it much
		- Party chooses to handle the issue later
			- "We can make more corpses" - [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/)
- Runs into lost dragonborn boy on the way back
	- boy seems to be drawn to [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/), who wants nothing to do with him
	- [Merla](/_party/merla/) offers him help and takes him back to the [Yawning Portal](/collections/_locations/Yawning Portal/), where he helps [Dilon](/_people/dilon/) work the bar
- Goes to see [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha/)
	- she is still distressed and begging for help for [Shirley](/_people/shirley/)
	- informs party that [Shirley](/_people/shirley/) is a noble from out of town, who visits a few times a year and stays with [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha/) when she does.
	- [Bertha le Gertha](/collections/_people/Bertha le Gertha/) promises that [Shirley](/_people/shirley/) will compensate the party if they save her
	- Party leaves and will make a decision after discussing the matter with [Felix Fizzler](/collections/_party/Felix Fizzler/)
	- Learn that she can be found in the trade ward where she works odd jobs and knows Greg
- Party returns to [Yawning Portal](/collections/_locations/Yawning Portal/) 
	- [Merla](/_party/merla/) asks [Dilon](/_people/dilon/) to provide the dragonborn boy a place to stay for the night
	- Party discusses framing [Shirley](/_people/shirley/) for crime
		- [Rhogar](/_party/rhogar/) is on board with framing her but not saving her
		- [Corvit Crowe](/collections/_party/Corvit Crowe/) proposes framing her so they will bring her out for execution and we can save her at that point, maybe easier than a prison break
		- [Merla](/_party/merla/) does not want to frame an innocent person, but wants to save [Shirley](/_people/shirley/)
