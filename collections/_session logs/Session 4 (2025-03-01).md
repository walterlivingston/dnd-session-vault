# The City Below
## Timeline
- Start in sewers
	- Felix Fizzler makes food for the party
	- Rest of party has a vision of 5 figures
		- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) describes vision to Felix Fizzler
			- figures are [Elminster](/People/Elminster/), [Mystra](/test/_gods/Mystra/) (pre-godhood), and [Kelemvor](/test/_gods/Kelemvor/), [Cyric](/test/_gods/Cyric/) and a Bhaal Worshipper who appears as a skeletal figure. 
		- [Echo](/People/Echo/) sees defiled prayers, painted over with blood, dead body to what may be east
			- body has symbol of [Ilmater](/test/_gods/Ilmater/) on it, with its colors correctly displayed, and [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) takes the pendant of [Ilmater](/test/_gods/Ilmater/) from the body.
			- Corvit Crowe finds his grocery list, full version of "eggs milk flour"
			- Felix Fizzler sees shaft that is above body
		- figure begins to approach us, its [Erron](/People/Erron/) who recognizes dead body and asks that [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) step away, which he does
			- [Erron](/People/Erron/) says it is a cleric of [Ilmater](/test/_gods/Ilmater/) named [Kath](/People/Kath/).
- Party exits sewer with [Erron](/People/Erron/), but are not anywhere they recognize
	- Appears to be North of City of the Dead, possibly North Ward
	- Start towards Hospice of St. Laupsenn, but party decides to head towards execution of [Shirley](/People/Shirley/), while [Erron](/People/Erron/) continues on
- Party heads towards north Castle Ward
	- Felix Fizzler transforms into gnome guard
	- [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) is in line to be executed, very short in stature, walking on all fours with fur, but wearing elegant regalia (reinforces that saving her would make us a lot of money)
		- She is a corgi, blink dog, a teleportation beast and she has special shackles
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) finds [Chua](/People/Chua/)'s parents and tells them to wait around in this area to see their son again.
	- Felix tells a Prisoner Goblin to run away, distracting the guards.
	- Felix Fizzler and Corvit Crowe end up saving [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) and leaving towards Bertha le Gertha's apartment.
	- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) is confronted by a guard and attacks.
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) launches his shortsword from below the execution stage, killing a guard and frees Prisoner Halfling.
	- Trying to help [Merla](/Party/Merla/), [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) flies into Unlucky Elfusing his cloak, sending the elf to the ground. 
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) then accidentally hits Unlucky Elf with fire breath instead of the guard.
	- Finally, [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) takes the head of the guard and throws it at the feet of the executioner, causing him to flee.
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) and [Merla](/Party/Merla/) finish fighting
		- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) takes [Chua](/People/Chua/)'s parents back to him at the Yawning Portal
		- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) goes to Bertha le Gertha's apartment
- At Bertha le Gertha's apartment
	- Felix Fizzler and Corvit Crowe have been gifted 100 gold and a baby corgi, who we name [Tilly](/People/Tilly/), one of [Shirley](/People/Shirley/)'s children.
	- They leave to find [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) and [Merla](/Party/Merla/)
- On the road to Bertha le Gertha's apartment
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/)  has a vision of Rhogar's Beloved given by [Sune](/test/_gods/Sune/). Also sees Rhogar's Parents appear as two dark red spots.
	- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) catches up with them after [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/)'s vision.
	- Corvit Crowe asks about what happened to [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) who denies seeing anything, but Felix Fizzler knows he had a vision.
	- Corvit Crowe gives [Merla](/Party/Merla/) and [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) 20 gold each.
	- [Erron](/People/Erron/) arrives
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) is astounded to receive such little reward for saving [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) and attempts to run after her.
	- [Erron](/People/Erron/) grabs [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) and as [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) fails to escape his grasp, he reveals hidden secrets and intentions about each of the party members, angering [Merla](/Party/Merla/) and [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/).
	
- Party returns to Yawning Portal
	- [Erron](/People/Erron/) is visibly upset about the loss of [Kath](/People/Kath/) and is very intoxicated.
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) attempts to sedate him with special tea leaves, but is caught and a brawl ensues.
	- Felix Fizzler attempts to mediate, but is shut down and instead flees to his bedroom.
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) hits [Erron](/People/Erron/) and watches as orcs join the fight, critically wounding both [Merla](/Party/Merla/) and Corvit Crowe. 
	- [Dilon](/People/Dilon/) helps [Merla](/Party/Merla/) up and she escapes behind the bar.
	- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) then makes an appeal to [Erron](/People/Erron/) who orders the orcs to stand down.
	- In an attempt to make peace, [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) gives [Kath](/People/Kath/)'s amulet to [Erron](/People/Erron/) and asks that the violence stop.
	- [Erron](/People/Erron/) accepts and the party finishes their night by going to sleep.
