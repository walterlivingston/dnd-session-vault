# Violence in Waterdeep

## Timeline
- Awake at the Yawning Portal
	- Opened gifts found under fir tree
	- Felix Fizzler is ill and Corvit Crowe slips his gift into his room
	- Corvit Crowe gets newspaper from [Dilon](/People/Dilon/), reads that many working to lower class families have been killed
- Party goes to the home of Bertha le Gertha
	- young child informs party Bertha le Gertha has gone to the market
- Travels to Trade Ward
	- busy street, party meets [Greg](/People/Greg/) the half-orc at Greg's Grogs
		- Corvit Crowe says we are servants of a paladin and asks for information about the massacre
		- informed that it was not done with magic and [Greg](/People/Greg/) believes it is the shadow thieves
		- learn that local watch has been pulled from the Trade Ward
- Go to location of massacre
	- nobody around, very bloody, lots of damage, explosive remnants
	- weapons, merch, and bodies, no valuable loot
	- Party is ambushed
		- after defeating them, party learns they were shadow thieves
			- take their robes/patches for possible use later
	- Party continues to investigate
		- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) finds sign with a symbol he recognizes on it
			- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) recognizes it as holy and gets similar feeling that she did when meeting [Erron](/People/Erron/)
		- Corvit Crowe sees symbol on wall that he does not recognize and neither does [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/)
	- Party sees manhole cover and chooses to go down
		- after running into a large monster strangely resembling the hungry hungry caterpillar, quickly go back up the ladder back into the square
		- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) watches as Corvit Crowe and [Merla](/Party/Merla/) feed the monster a poisoned corpse, does not seem to affect it much
		- Party chooses to handle the issue later
			- "We can make more corpses" - Corvit Crowe
- Runs into lost dragonborn boy on the way back
	- boy seems to be drawn to [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/), who wants nothing to do with him
	- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) offers him help and takes him back to the Yawning Portal, where he helps [Dilon](/People/Dilon/) work the bar
- Goes to see Bertha le Gertha
	- she is still distressed and begging for help for [Shirley](/People/Shirley/)
	- informs party that [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) is a noble from out of town, who visits a few times a year and stays with Bertha le Gertha when she does.
	- Bertha le Gertha promises that [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) will compensate the party if they save her
	- Party leaves and will make a decision after discussing the matter with Felix Fizzler
	- Learn that she can be found in the trade ward where she works odd jobs and knows Greg
- Party returns to Yawning Portal 
	- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) asks [Dilon](/People/Dilon/) to provide the dragonborn boy a place to stay for the night
	- Party discusses framing [Shirley](/People/Shirley/) for crime
		- [Rhogar](/Party/Rhogar/) is on board with framing her but not saving her
		- Corvit Crowe proposes framing her so they will bring her out for execution and we can save her at that point, maybe easier than a prison break
		- [Merla](/Party/Merla/) does not want to frame an innocent person, but wants to save [Shirley](/People/Shirley/)
